Emergency Plumbing FAQs

What qualifies as a plumbing emergency?

Active flooding, burst pipes, sewage backup, complete water loss, gas leaks near plumbing, or overflowing toilets. If unsure, WhatsApp us—we'll triage instantly.

Is the callout fee charged if you can't fix it?

Yes. The dispatch fee covers travel, diagnostics, and on-site assessment. It applies regardless of outcome. Full work quote provided before repair begins.

Do you work nights/weekends/holidays?

Yes. 24/7 dispatch. Standard hourly rates apply. After-hours surcharge (+40%) only applies outside 8AM–6PM Mon–Sat or on public holidays.

What if I need specialized parts?

Techs carry common emergency parts. If specialized components are needed, we secure a temporary fix or source them within 24 hours. You're only billed for time spent.
