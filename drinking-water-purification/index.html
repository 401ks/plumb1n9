What's the difference between RO, UV & carbon filtration?

RO removes dissolved salts, heavy metals & micro-contaminants. UV destroys bacteria/viruses. Carbon removes chlorine taste, odors & organic compounds. We test your water first to recommend the right combination for your specific concerns. Keywords: RO vs UV vs carbon filter colombo

How often do filters need replacement?

Depends on system and usage: sediment filters every 3-6 months, carbon filters every 6-12 months, RO membranes every 2-3 years, UV lamps annually. We provide a maintenance schedule and offer replacement services. Keywords: water filter replacement schedule colombo

Do you guarantee the purification system performance?

Yes. 6-month water quality guarantee on all drinking water purification installations. If the system fails to remove targeted contaminants within 6 months due to installation, we return free. Pay after service guarantee. Keywords: water purifier warranty colombo
