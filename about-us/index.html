About Six7 Plumbing – Built Around One Simple Idea: Do The Job Properly

Most people don't think about plumbers until something goes wrong. A leaking pipe, a blocked drain, a broken water heater, or an emergency flooding situation usually sends homeowners searching for help fast.

The problem is that finding a plumber and finding a good plumber are not always the same thing.

At Six7 Plumbing, we believe customers deserve clear communication, honest recommendations, quality workmanship, and solutions that actually solve the problem instead of temporarily hiding it. That philosophy guides every service call we take.

Why We Started

The plumbing industry often suffers from a trust problem. Many homeowners have experienced:

  • Repairs that didn't last
  • Unexpected charges
  • Poor communication
  • Missed appointments
  • Temporary fixes instead of proper solutions

We built Six7 Plumbing with a different approach. Our goal is straightforward: Diagnose the problem correctly, explain it clearly, and fix it properly. No unnecessary upselling. No confusing technical jargon. No pressure. Just practical plumbing solutions backed by professional workmanship.

What We Do

We provide residential and commercial plumbing services throughout Colombo and surrounding areas. Our services include:

  • Emergency plumbing repairs
  • Drain cleaning and blockage removal
  • Leak detection and repair
  • Water heater services
  • Water purification solutions
  • Pipe repairs and replacements
  • Bathroom and kitchen plumbing
  • Preventive plumbing maintenance

Whether it's a small repair or a major plumbing issue, we approach every job with the same attention to detail.

Our Approach

  • 1. Inspect First – Before recommending repairs, we identify the actual cause of the problem. Treating symptoms without addressing the source often leads to recurring issues.
  • 2. Explain Clearly – We believe customers should understand what is happening with their plumbing system. We explain the issue, discuss available solutions, and answer questions before work begins.
  • 3. Provide Honest Recommendations – Not every problem requires the most expensive solution. Our recommendations are based on what your plumbing system needs, not what generates the highest invoice.
  • 4. Complete The Work Properly – We focus on long-term reliability rather than quick temporary fixes. The goal is to leave customers with a plumbing system that performs as expected after we leave.

Our Values

  • Integrity – We believe trust is earned through actions, not advertising.
  • Reliability – When customers contact us, they expect solutions, not excuses.
  • Transparency – Clear communication and honest pricing should be standard, not optional.
  • Quality Workmanship – The quality of a repair is measured by how well it performs long after the job is completed.

More Than Just Repairs

Good plumbing is about more than pipes and fittings. It affects health, safety, water quality, property protection, and daily comfort. A properly functioning plumbing system helps homes and businesses operate without disruption. That's why we take every project seriously, regardless of size.
