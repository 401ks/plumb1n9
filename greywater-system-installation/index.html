Wasting Clean Water on Gardens & Toilets?

In Colombo's water-stressed environment, using potable water for irrigation or toilet flushing is wasteful and expensive. Greywater systems capture water from sinks, showers, and laundry for reuse – reducing bills and supporting conservation.
