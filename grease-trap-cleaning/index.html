Professional Grease Trap Cleaning in Colombo

Is Your Grease Trap Due for Cleaning or Causing Blockages?

Grease traps fill with fats, oils, and food waste over time. When not cleaned regularly, they overflow, cause sewer blockages, create foul odors, and violate health codes. Colombo's strict municipal regulations require commercial kitchens to maintain documented grease trap cleaning schedules. Professional cleaning prevents emergencies, fines, and business disruption.
