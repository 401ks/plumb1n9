Why is whole house filter installation priced higher than standard plumbing?

The LKR 12,000 + LKR 5,400/hr rate reflects this specialized service. Callout fee applies to all dispatches regardless of outcome. Keywords: whole house filter cost colombo

What contaminants can a whole house filter remove?

Depending on system configuration: hardness minerals (calcium/magnesium), iron/manganese, sediment, chlorine, bad tastes/odors, and some heavy metals. We test your water first to recommend the right filtration stages for your specific concerns. Keywords: water filter contaminants colombo

How often do filter media need replacement?

Depends on water quality and usage: sediment filters every 3-6 months, carbon filters every 6-12 months, softening/iron media every 2-5 years. We provide a maintenance schedule and offer replacement services. Keywords: water filter maintenance colombo

Do you guarantee the filtration system performance?

Yes. 6-month water quality guarantee on all whole house filter installations. If the system fails to remove targeted contaminants within 6 months due to installation, we return free. Pay after service guarantee. Keywords: water filter warranty colombo

Can you install filters in older Colombo properties?

Absolutely. We specialize in older plumbing systems common in Colombo – working around legacy pipes, limited space, and heritage constraints while ensuring effective, code-compliant filtration installation. Keywords: old property water filter colombo
